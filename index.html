F.A.Q.

"How long does shipping take?"
1-2 weeks standard USPS shipping.
"What comes with a zine pack?"
You will get all 5 zines plus a free random zine & 5 random stickers.
"What does the money go towards?"
50% of all sales will go towards purchasing supplies for Food not Bombs D.C. The other 50% goes towards the operational costs of the distro (shipping, shipping supplies, stickers, paper, printer toner). We don't make any money from this, in fact we personally operate at a loss.


"Can I have this shipped internationally??"
Unfortunately the cost and complication of getting certain items thru customs is annoying, so no.
