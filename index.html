F.A.Q.

"How long does shipping take?"
1-2 weeks standard USPS shipping.
"What comes with a zine pack?"
You will get all 5 zines plus a free random zine & a prisoner letting writing kit (paper + envelope + stamp) and a pack of 36 stickers (6 sheets).
"What does the money go towards?"
50% of all sales will go towards purchasing supplies for Food not Bombs D.C. The other 50% goes towards the operational costs of the distro (shipping, shipping supplies, stickers, paper, printer toner).
