F.A.Q.

"How long does shipping take?"
1-2 weeks standard USPS shipping.
"What comes with a zine pack?"
You will get all 5 zines plus a free zine and a pack of 36 stickers (6 sheets).
"What does the money go towards?"
50% of all sales will go towards purchasing supplies for Food not Bombs D.C. The other 50% goes towards the operational costs of the distro (shipping, shipping supplies, stickers, paper, printer toner).

    A collection of communiques from the Ferguson revolts in 2014, along with Jackie Wang's pivotal Against Innocence, a scathing critique of respectability politics and carceral society.
