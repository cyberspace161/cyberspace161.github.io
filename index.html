/// TIC

A take-down of capitalist cybernetics, offering instead a revitalized hacker ethic (from To Our Friends). Three more texts from The Invisible Committee's Now, and finally a real proposal for armed joy in the 21st century. contains:

  • Fuck Off Google by The Invisible Committee
  • Everybody Hates the Police by The Invisible Committee
  • Tomorrow is Canceled by The Invisible Committee
  • Let's Destitute the World by The Invisible Committee
  • Beyond the Sad Comforts and Stale Air of Radicalism by Carla Bergman and Nick Montgomery

    • /// WORLD_IN_REVOLT

    Dispatches from insurrections all around the world. A primer on autonomy, and two texts from Phil A. Neel. contains:

  • The People Want the Fall of the Regime by Various Authors
  • Memes with Force by Paul Torino
  • Inhabit by Various Authors
  • Oaths of Blood, Oaths of Water by Phil A. Neel
  • Why Riot? by Phil A. Neel

    • /// BLACK_AUTONOMY

    A collection of communiques from the Ferguson revolts in 2014, along with Jackie Wang's pivotal Against Innocence, a scathing critique of respectability politics and carceral society. Two classic texts from Kuwasi Balagoon (R.I.P.) written from prison and an anonymous declaration of Black Anarchy. contains:

  • Dispatches from Ferguson vol. 1 by Anti State STL
  • The Incomprehensible Black Anarchist Position by Anonymous
  • Against Innocence by Jackie Wang
  • Brink's Trial Closing Statement by Kuwasi Balagoon
  • Anarchy Can't Fight Alone by Kuwasi Balagoon

    • /// J20

    A collection of communiques from the fateful day in 2017. Contains multiple (constructive) critiques of wider Anarchist strategy and tactics. All anonymously written and formatted by Crimethinc. contains:

  • Lift Every Rift With Ore
  • Anarchist Resistance to the Trump Inauguration
  • We've Got Your Back
  • I was a J20 Street Medic and Defendant
  • Between the Sun and the Sea
